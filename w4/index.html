obsession, as with some others, the heavy moebius inspiration in its designs and direction
combined with the passion shown in it's production and how it expands on

easily draw me into anything.

LUPIN THE 3RD

Lupin is a long running series that I've been very lightly into. I keep meaning to
watch more of the movies but I often forget to. The movies contain heavily varied
tones and stories, which makes them a bit hard to explain since characters can act
in completely different ways based on the movie, adhering to a few base traits,
Lupin himself in particular ranging from a straightforward hero in some movies
to a villain who's only followed because his name is on the cover in some others.
The best way I can describe them is like a strange cross between James Bond and
Scooby Doo.
While Cagliostro is the most popular movie in the series, I'm very fond of
The Mystery Of Mamo, the movie immediately preceding it. Though, as I've said before,
I've not really watched much of the movies in total...
